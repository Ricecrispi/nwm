Game Objective

In the game, your task is to match aliens that share similar properties. The object of the game is to produce optimal groupings—groupings that have the highest degree of similarity.

What makes aliens similar?

For aliens to be similar, they have to share identical properties (i.e. if two aliens have the exact same bracelet, they are considered similar). Aliens that share similar but not identical properties are not considered similar (i.e. if two aliens are wearing bracelets, but the bracelets are different, they are not considered similar unless they have other features in common).
