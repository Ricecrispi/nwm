About Matchmakers

Matchmakers is a serious game ("game with a purpose") that uses human visual cognition in solving “n-way model merging”; a problem that computers cannot efficiently solve. N-way model merging (nwm) is the process of combining several models of software into one. It is considered an important aspect of software engineering. Some of its applications include combining opinions from multiple stakeholders, and merging products in a product line.
