In the game, your goal is to help the lonely alien in the top panel form a clique. In this tutorial, you will be helping #9. A strong clique is one in which its aliens share many physical properties. Physical properties can be accessories, facial features, hair, apparel, etc. There are a total of 162 properties and you may view them all here. The stronger the similarily between the members of your clique, the higher its score will be. In order to win the game, your clique must reach the target score shown in the pink box in the bottom right corner of the screen.
In the game, other aliens might've already formed their own cliques. Aliens in the same clique appear in boxes with the same colored border. For instance, #1 and #5 both have a blue border color so they form a clique.
Aliens that aren't in cliques are in boxes with no border, like #6.
The red bar below each alien indicates the strength of its bond with its clique. A full bar means that it loves its clique and is less willing to leave, while an empty bar means that the alien dislikes its clique and is more willing to leave. Sometimes, it may be beneficial for you to add physicially similar aliens with a weak bond to your clique, since they have a high chance of forming a strong bond.
Aliens that are physically similar to your clique are highlighted with a yellow background, like #1, #3, #4, and #6. Adding these aliens to your clique have the most potential to increase your score. Left click on an alien to add it to your clique. #4 is similar to #9, and may increase your score, add to your clique #4 now.
