The goal of this game is to group together similar aliens. Aliens are similar if they share identical properties such as facial features, hair, shirt patterns, etc. You may view the set of all alien properties here. The best groupings, in which aliens of the same group are as similar as possible, are awarded the highest scores. In the game, you will be presented with the current best combination of groupings created by previous players. Your goal is to beat the existing combination's score to the best of your ability. Have fun!

You may create new groups by clicking on the “Create new group” button in the bottom right panel. When you start the game, a new group is already made for you.

Click on an alien to add it to your currently selected group. You may view the aliens in your current group at the top panel. Click on any alien now.
Enemies of aliens in your currently selected group are in striped boxes. Enemy aliens cannot be grouped together. If you choose to add such aliens to a group, you will be prompted to remove its enemy. Click on an enemy alien now.
Aliens that are similar to your current group appear in yellow boxes. Adding these aliens to your group are more likely to increase your score. Your score can still go down if you add yellow aliens to your group, if the alien was already in a group that resulted in a higher score, or if adding the alien caused its enemy, which may have been a better match , to be removed from the group. Add a yellow alien to your current group now.
To remove an alien from a group, click on it on the top panel. Remove an alien from your current group now.
If you would like to make changes to an existing group, right click on any alien from that group. Right click on an existing group now.
