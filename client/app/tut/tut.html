The goal of this game is to group together similar aliens. Aliens are similar if they share identical properties. Properties can be facial features, hair, apparel, etc. You may view the full set of all alien properties here. In the game, you will be presented with the current best combination of groupings created by previous players. Your goal is to beat the current best score by coming up with even better groupings.
Groupings are represented by colored borders around the aliens. Aliens in the same group have a border of the same color. For instance, #2 and #9 both have a pink background color so they are in the same group.
The red bar below each alien indicates how similar it is to other member of the group. A full bar means that it is very similar, while an empty bar means that the alien isn't very similar. In the game, try to increase the percentage bars of each alien by coming up with more optimal groupings.
To modify a group, right click on any alien inside the group you want to change. The group you selected will appear in the top panel. Right click on the pink group now.
Aliens that share many similar features to your selected group are highlighted with a yellow background, like #1, #3, #4, and #6. These aliens have potential to increase your score if you add them to your group.
