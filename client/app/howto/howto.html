Game Objective

In the game, your task is to match aliens that share similar properties. The object of the game is to produce optimal groupings—groupings that have the highest degree of similarity.

Here is another alien that is similar to the first alien. They both have a red diamond.

Here is another alien that is even more similar to the first alien. They both have the same tail and hairstyle. This pair makes up a more optimal grouping that the previous pair. In the game, you want to match aliens that are as similar as possible.


What makes aliens similar?

For aliens to be similar, they have to share identical properties (i.e. if two aliens have the exact same bracelet, they are considered similar). Aliens that share similar but not identical properties are not considered similar (i.e. if two aliens are wearing bracelets, but the bracelets are different, they are not considered similar unless they have other features in common).

Game Walk-through

1) Choose a game setting. You can either play from the current best solution, in which the game will be pre-loaded with groupings from the current best solution, continue from your previously saved state, or start from scratch.


2) Scroll through the game screen to look for aliens that share similar properties.


3) Create a new group. (NOTE: When you start the game, a new empty group is already made for your so you don’t have to make one yourself).

You can see what your current group is in the small box below “CURRENT GROUP”, which displays the color of your current group.


4) Add aliens to a group by clicking on them. Aliens from the same group will have the same colored background.


5) Remove an alien from a grouping by clicking on it again.


6) Aliens that are similar to your current group are highlighted with a yellow border.


You can also view these aliens all at once on an adjustable side panel.

8) View your score.


9) Undo/redo your actions.


10) End game (to either save your game for later, submit your score, or quit the game without saving).
